# China-related PQC Card

## Scope

This card covers domestic or China-related material in the uploaded corpus: Aigis, Scloud+, CTRU, NEV, LAC-related material, NTRU-based KEM consultation material, and PQC-in-China presentations.

## Use for

- explaining domestic PQC research directions;
- teaching that PQC standardization has both international and domestic ecosystems;
- discussing candidate algorithm families when directly sourced.

## Do not use for

- claiming NIST FIPS status;
- claiming Chinese national/industry standard status unless a primary standardization document is added;
- exact parameter or benchmark claims unless extracted from a primary table.
